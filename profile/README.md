## Trust infrastructure for the AI agent economy.

AgenticTrust builds the boring-but-critical back-office that AI agents need to act with authority, spend responsibly, and earn trust.

### Products

🛡️ **[Agent Authority Vault](https://agentauthority.dev)** — Delegated authority for AI agents. Power of attorney as an API. Define what your agents can do, verify before they act, revoke instantly.

💰 **[Safe-Spend](https://safe-spend.dev)** — Fiat-first escrow and spending controls for AI agents. Give your agents a budget with guardrails, not raw wallet access.
